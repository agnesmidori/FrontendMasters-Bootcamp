How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get 2 pieces of bread
  2. Get peanut butter
  3. Get the jelly
  4. Get a knife
  5. Take one part of the bread
  6. Take a portion of peanut butter with the knife
  7. Spread the peanut butter with the knife in one piece of bread
  8. Repeat the 2 steps before until it's enough for your taste
  9. Take a portion of jelly with the knife
  10. Spread the jelly with the knife in the other piece of bread
  11. Repeat the 2 steps before until it's enough for your taste
  12. Put the peanut butter and jelly facing each other.
  13. It's read to eat! Enjoy the peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Professor's Solution

  1. Get some peanut butter, jelly and 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread and jelly on the other.
  3. Put the peanut butter and jelly together, with the bread side facing out.
  4. Eat and enjoy!
